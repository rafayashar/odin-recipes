Northwest Steakhouse Steak Marinade

Description

This Pan-Seared Steak has a garlic rosemary-infused butter that makes it taste steakhouse quality. You’ll be impressed at how easy it is to make the perfect steak – seared and caramelized on the outside, and so juicy inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch seasoned salt
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crushed
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Stir the seasoning salt, garlic powder, Greek seasoning, and bouillon cube into the melted butter.
  2. Mix until the bouillon cube dissolves.
  3. Pour the marinade into a wide and shallow dish and place a steak of your choice into the marinade, turning to coat both sides. Cook or grill as desired.
